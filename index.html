Welcome To JSW!

Welcome to JSW Event Management, a premier event management company dedicated to creating extraordinary experiences that leave a lasting impression.

JSW Event Management was founded in 2022 with its head office in Johor Malaysia, specializing in human resource management via event management.

We provide a full range of services including event management ,workforce supply, and event marketing. Our services cover a wide range of activities in Nationwide Malaysia and Singapore.

Services


Event Management

Event management involves the planning, organization, and execution of various events. It includes defining event objectives, budgeting, venue selection, logistics, vendor management, marketing, registration, on-site coordination, attendee experience, and post-event evaluation. Event managers oversee all aspects to ensure a successful event that meets goals and provides a positive experience for attendees.

Manpower Recruitment Services

We analyze, forecast, and plan workforce supply and demand, assessing gaps, and determining target talent management interventions to ensure that an organization has the right people with the right skills in the right places at the right time to fulfill its mandate and strategic objectives.

Event Marketing

Event marketing is the process of promoting an event to attract attendees. It involves using various marketing channels such as social media, email, content marketing, and collaborations with influencers and partners to create awareness, generate interest, and drive attendance. The goal is to effectively communicate the event's value proposition, build excitement, and engage the target audience to ensure a successful event.
