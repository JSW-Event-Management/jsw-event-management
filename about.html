About Us

JSW Event Management was founded in 2022 with its head office in Johor, Malaysia, specializing in human resource management and event management. Our services cover a wide range of activities Nationwide around Malaysia and Singapore.

We provide a full range of services including:

  • Event Management
  • Manpower Recruitment Services
  • Event Marketing

As part of our commitment to providing the greatest event management services, we have a team of professionals to ensure that your event runs smoothly and successfully.
